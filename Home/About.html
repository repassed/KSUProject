About

Previously CPR certified and climbing certified, multiple experiences with multi-million dollar military assets for identifying aircraft and weather phenomena.

His passion for computers has led me to be constantly intrigued by the wonders of the digital world. As it is an ever expanding horizon to be developed and explored.
